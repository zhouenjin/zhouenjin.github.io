About

A homepage that reflects both study and practice.

My recent work sits at the intersection of mathematical thinking, algorithm intuition, and implementation practice. I like building compact learning systems: notes that turn scattered material into a map, scripts that make ideas observable, and projects that let theory become something concrete.

Right now, that shows up in three directions. First, a reinforcement learning note system that organizes methods from MDPs to PPO. Second, embodied AI experiments in Isaac Sim, starting from scene setup, robot kinematics, and tool-center pose reasoning. Third, simulation notes that walk from PBD and XPBD to FEM with runnable code and visual comparisons.
